Nea Springer

Gaffer and Spark

Brighton / London | 27 years old

When Film is at its best it shapes the perspectives through which we see the world. To me, there are so many new unseen angles to explore. I'd say I'm passionate for that creativity and the tech that makes it happen.

I work on a variety of forms, but predominantly in narrative and music video, but I also do some fashion, doc and various online content work. I love a mix!

Exercising control over ambient light, finding the right key angle and quality for subjects is stuff I cherish - and then on a good day there's play with solids and positives to shape the subject and the world around them.

I love following my DPs on the way they see light tightly, that collaboration; and bringing our ways together - those are the best days - when we're both excited by how those photons bounce around!

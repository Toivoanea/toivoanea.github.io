Nea Springer

Gaffer and pastime Screenwriter

Brighton / London | 25 years old

Film has this incredible power to shape the way we think about the world, which inspired my journey to the medium where I also fulfil my passions for creativity and tech.

I work on a variety of forms, but predominantly in narrative and music video, yet any shoot provides new fun challenges for me to face.

I love exercising control over ambient light and finding the right key light angle and quality for subjects - then using solids and positives to shape the subject and the world around them.

I love following my DPs on the way they see light tightly, bringing our ways together - the best days are the ones on which we're both excited by those photons!

Feel free to drop me an email at NeaSpringer@gmail.com or have a look at my CV.
